OUR FEATURES

Track your revenues, you can have records of all your revenues along with their date and amounts. Statistics will be calculated as well by combining these revenues with your expenses to show you a graphical representation of your entities.

Track your expenses, you can have records of all your expenses along with their date and amounts. Statistics will be calculated as well by combining these expenses with your revenues to show you a graphical representation of your entities.

Track your net profit, a statistical graph can be visible to show you how far your project is going. You can also set milestones to reach a certain target for your net profit.

Search, Edit, or Delete older entries, you can make a lot of modifications to your older entries or search in your projects by an expense name for example.
